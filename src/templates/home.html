World View

Click a country to select your card currency. The fill-colour of other countries will indicate the payment scheme with the lower average rate last week. Red indicates that Mastercard has the cheaper rate whereas blue indicates that Visa has the cheaper rate. The opacity of the fill-colour is based on the percentile-rank of the relative rate. The labels on the scale indicate in basis points (1% of 1%) how much cheaper the preffered scheme is than the competitor.
